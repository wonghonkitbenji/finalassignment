NTU Spirit!

NTU Canoe Polo (NTUCP) is an established CCA under the Nanyang Technological University. It is a competitive team that participates in many national sporting events such as the annual NTU Opens, SP Opens and Singapore Canoe Polo Championships

Singapore Canoe Federation

To promote opportunity for public participation in a full range of canoeing activities in Singapore with high standards of safety.


The Singapore Canoe Federation (SCF) was founded in 1971 and is the National Sports Association responsible for the management, coordination, development and promotion of canoeing in Singapore. Canoe Polo is one of the many disciplines of Canoeing.

As a non-profit organisation, the SCF relies mainly on its volunteers and its small group of staff. It is mainly funded through Sport Singapore.
