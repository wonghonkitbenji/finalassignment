CANOE POLO

Intense!

Canoe Polo is an exciting and challenging sports that require the skills of kayaking and water polo. The high intensity sport is typically played by 2 teams of 5 players on a rectangular playing pitch that can be in open water or in a swimming pool. The aim of the game is to score into goals that are suspended above water at each end of the pitch and getting as many goals as possible within the match time.

It is fast paced, aggressive and exhilarating! Canoe polo is definitely the sport to make you feel adrenaline rushes and never been more alive than ever before!

Gameplay and Rules

Join us!


Each team typically has five players on the pitch, who would compete to score goals in their opponents' goal that are suspended two metres above the water. The ball can be thrown by hand or flicked with the paddle to pass between players and shoot at the goal. The pitch can be set up in swimming pools or any stretch of flat water, which should measure 35 metres by 23 metres.

The game is played as a 14 - 20 minute game consisting of two 7 - 10 minute halves. The teams change ends at the half time period, which is 1 to 3 minutes long. Each half begins with a "sprint" where an internally designated player would contest for the possession of the ball, when it is thrown into the middle of the pitch by the referee.
